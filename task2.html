About Benedict Cumberbatch

Benedict Cumberbatch is a British actor and film producer. He is best known for his roles in television, film, and theatre, and has received numerous awards and nominations, including a BAFTA, a Primetime Emmy Award, and a Laurence Olivier Award.

Important Events

  1. 1976 – Born in London, England.
  2. 2001 – Began his professional acting career.
  3. 2010 – Breakthrough role as Sherlock Holmes in "Sherlock".
  4. 2014 – Nominated for an Academy Award for "The Imitation Game"..
  5. 2022 – Won a BAFTA for Best Actor in "The Power of the Dog".

Key Contributions

  • Portrayal of Sherlock Holmes
  • Role as Doctor Strange in the Marvel Cinematic Universe
  • Portrayal of Alan Turing in "The Imitation Game"
  • Extensive work in theatre and voice acting
